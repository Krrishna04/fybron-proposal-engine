    Fybron Scope of Supply & Work

    Pool Supply & Delivery

    • Supply and deliver the approved factory-manufactured Fybron fibreglass pool shell/panel system as per approved specifications.
    • Safe transportation and handling of all supplied materials.
    • Minor handling marks during transportation or installation shall be rectified.

    Pool Finish

    • Interior: smooth premium pigmented gelcoat finish for durability and easy maintenance.
    • Exterior: protective flow-coat finish for enhanced structural durability.

    MEP Works

    • Supply and installation of pool circulation pump and filtration system.
    • PVC pipelines and fittings, underwater lights if included in BOQ, and electrical control panel connections.

    Installation, Testing & Handover

    • Pool positioning, installation, and on-site technical supervision.
    • Testing and commissioning of the complete pool system.
    • Handover of the pool in fully functional and ready-to-use condition.

    Client / Contractor Scope

    Site Preparation & Civil Works

    • Site clearing, excavation, and disposal of excavated material.
    • Construction of minimum 100 mm RCC/PCC pool bed/foundation as per approved drawings.
    • Retaining walls, structural supports, M-sand, and approved backfilling materials where required.

    Utilities & Site Arrangements

    • Electrical power supply and electrical points inside the pump room.
    • Adequate water supply for testing and pool filling.
    • Pump room construction and waterproof flooring where applicable.

    Finishing Works

    • Pool decking, coping, external finishing, aesthetic works, and landscaping.
    • External stairs, pathways, cladding, changing rooms, and showers if applicable.

    Coordination & Site Management

    • Coordination with civil contractors and related agencies.
    • Timely completion of preparatory works to maintain project schedule.
    • Site access arrangements, labour coordination, and local permissions.
